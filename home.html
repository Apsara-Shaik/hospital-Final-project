USEFUL OF ONLINE REGISTRATION

Convenience: Patients can register or update their information from home at any time

Time Savings: allowing patients to spend less time in the waiting room.

Reduced Errors: reducing the likelihood of errors compared to handwritten forms.

Streamlined Process: Healthcare providers can manage patient records more efficiently

Improved Accessibility: Patients can register from anywhere, which is especially beneficial who live in remote areas.

Enhanced Communication: Online systems can facilitate easier communication between patients and healthcare providers

Cost Efficiency: Reduces the administrative costs associated with managing physical paperwork and manual data entry.
